Privacy Policy – Sport Club Scheduler

Effective Date: 2026-03-20

Developer: Samkarikalan

Contact: samkarikalan@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Sport Club Scheduler is a session management tool for badminton and pickleball clubs. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Player names — entered by organisers to set up sessions
  • Session data — round results, scores, wins and losses
  • Player ratings — calculated based on session performance
  • Club information — club name and membership
  • Email address — used only for OTP verification when creating a club
  • Subscription status — to manage your trial and active subscription
  • Device installation date — to track free trial period (stored locally)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate scheduling and session management features
  • To display live session scores to all club members
  • To calculate and display player ratings and statistics
  • To verify club creation via one-time password (OTP)
  • To manage subscription status and free trial

4. Cloud Storage (Supabase)

The App uses Supabase (a secure cloud database) to store club and session data, allowing real-time sharing across devices. Supabase complies with GDPR and industry security standards.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing (Android) and Apple In-App Purchases (iOS). We do not collect or store any payment card details. All billing is handled securely by Google and Apple.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except:

  • Supabase — cloud database provider
  • Google / Apple — for subscription billing only

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local device data is removed when the app is uninstalled. Cloud data is retained as long as your club account exists. To request deletion, contact samkarikalan@gmail.com.

8. Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Club accounts are protected by organiser-set passwords.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact: samkarikalan@gmail.com

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
